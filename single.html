Why plastic recycling matters ?

The world produces millions of tons of plastic waste every year, with a large portion of that ends up polluting the environment. When plastic isn't recycled, it can take hundreds of years to decompose, causing long-lasting harm to the environment. However, recycling offers a powerful way to address this issue. Plastic recycling benefits include: Reducing Pollution: Recycling plastic reduces the need for new plastic production, which in turn cuts down on the pollution caused by plastic manufacturing. Conserving Resources: By reusing plastic materials, we conserve precious resources like oil and natural gas, which are used to produce virgin plastics. Minimizing Landfill Waste: Recycling diverts plastic waste from landfills, reducing the strain on these limited spaces and decreasing the need for more land to be allocated for waste disposal.
